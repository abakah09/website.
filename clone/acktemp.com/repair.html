 Residential Service for Heating and Cooling Equipment

Akl-Temp is the respected company in the northwest suburbs for handling service of residential heating and cooling equipment.

How do you know if you need to request a service call? It’s easy. You need to request a service call if your heating or cooling equipment is not functioning as it should function. Once you discover a potential problem, you should call us right away because a small problem may quickly turn into a larger problem if it is not addressed in a timely manner. Do not try to fix the problem yourself, as you may cause further damage to the equipment or even injure yourself. Our technicians have the training and knowledge to handle your heating and cooling problems quickly and safely.

With decades of experience, the professional team at Akl-Temp Mechanical Systems is able to take care of any service need that you may have for your heating and cooling equipment.

Sometimes, a problem requires immediate attention. For example, if your air conditioning fails on a very hot day, or if your furnace fails on a very cold day, you need to call us right away because these are potentially life-threatening situations. If you have a service emergency, call our office at any time, day or night, at (847) 719-2665. One of our trained professionals will respond to your call and take the necessary steps to restore your equipment to proper working order.
Even if your heating and cooling equipment is functional, you can always contact us to request a Precision Tune-Up. These tune-ups will extend the life of your equipment and prevent the need for unnecessary repairs.
