Commercial Installation

Akl-Temp Mechanical Systems is the perfect choice to design and build your new commercial heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system.

We have the advantage of an in-house design and build team to make sure that your installation is performed in a timely and cost-efficient manner. Sometimes contractors will hire subcontractors to handle various aspects of a project. This can create problems with scheduling, material acquisition, and design implementation. The subcontractors may prioritize other projects, purchase substandard materials, or perform shoddy work. You want to be able to trust the people who are installing your new HVAC system, and because our talented craftsmen work only for us, they are dedicated to your project from the initial planning stages to the final installation.

We have handled installation projects for many different kinds of commercial buildings, so you can trust that we will be familiar with the general HVAC issues that are common for each kind of building. However, we also know that each building is unique, so we are flexible in our design approach, and we are willing to consider innovative ways of delivering maximum comfort for your business.

Saving energy has never been easier with the availability of direct digital control systems. These computerized systems help to reduce the inefficiencies caused by manual operation by automatically regulating conditions such as light, temperature, and humidity. You can set different conditions for different areas of your business, and you can also monitor how energy is being used in real time. Akl-Temp has plenty of experience with all the major suppliers of control systems, so you can select the one that is right for you.
